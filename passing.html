Passing

Get to the ball early. Finish before transition.

  • Body position first. Set early angles before the ball arrives.
  • Simple movement. Footwork that gets you stopped and balanced.
  • Finish before transition. Complete the pass, then go.
  • Own the seams. 1–6 seam and 5–6 seam are non-negotiable.
  • Platform to target. Every pass moves the ball toward the setter zone.
