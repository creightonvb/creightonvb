Defense

Read the hitter. Trust the call. Dig with intent — not just a touch.

  • Eyes through the seam. Hitter → block → ball.
  • Base sets the table — every defense flows from a clean base.
  • Diggers behind the block, not next to it.
  • Vs. RS: match the look — double or 1-on-1, the call dictates.
  • Cover the line until proven otherwise.
