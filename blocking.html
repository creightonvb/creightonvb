Blocking

Middle blockers — the document below breaks down our blocking system. The clips show it in action.

  • Fronting — MB stays responsible for their middle and is in front of their MH at set.
  • Neutral — Middle stays neutral at time of set and our right-side block helps more against 3.
  • Commit — On a perfect pass, our MB jumps and commits to blocking their middle, giving a 1-on-1 vs. the outside hitter. Used more vs. stud middles or freeball situations.
