Leave Nothing

The standard. No regret, no excuse, no piece of yourself left on the bench.

  • G — Greatness. Pursue the highest version of yourself every rep.
  • R — Resilience. Bounce back faster than the moment that knocked you down.
  • I — Intent. Every drill, every meeting, every conversation has a purpose.
  • T — Trust. Earn it with consistency. Give it freely once it's earned.
